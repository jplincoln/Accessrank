ORIGIN STORY

Fix the code.
Own the rank.

JP Lincoln, the founder of Crier Communications — a Los Angeles public relations, social media, and website development agency — has worked with a Lima, Peru-based software and SEO development team led by Carlos Maruffo for nearly two decades. When one of Crier's clients got hit with a predatory compliance lawsuit, Carlos and his team sprang into action, pushing that client's accessibility score high enough to discourage any future lawyers from looking their way again.

In the process, they discovered the velocity of ADA lawsuits being filed nationwide is truly staggering, with over 95% of websites unusable to people with visual, hearing, mobility, or cognitive impairments — that's 1 in 4 Americans living with a disability. Beyond business-crushing lawsuits — everyone should have easy access to what the web has to offer.

ADA-compliant websites also rank higher than non-compliant ones. Which makes sense: if a page is fully readable by a blind patron using assistive software, Google and AI searches can read it too. Access and ranking go together.

That was the aha moment that led to Accessrank.
